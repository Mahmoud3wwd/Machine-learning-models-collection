Here's a list of common machine learning models along with their typical evaluation metrics:

### Classification Models

1. **Logistic Regression**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

2. **Support Vector Machine (SVM)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

3. **K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

4. **Naive Bayes**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

5. **Decision Tree**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

6. **Random Forest**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

7. **Gradient Boosting Machines (e.g., XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

8. **Neural Networks (e.g., MLP)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

9. **AdaBoost**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC, Confusion Matrix

### Regression Models

1. **Linear Regression**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

2. **Ridge Regression**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

3. **Lasso Regression**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

4. **Elastic Net**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

5. **Support Vector Regressor (SVR)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

6. **Decision Tree Regressor**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

7. **Random Forest Regressor**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

8. **Gradient Boosting Regressor (e.g., XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

9. **Neural Networks (e.g., MLP Regressor)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

10. **AdaBoost Regressor**
    - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), R-squared

### Clustering Models

1. **K-Means**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Silhouette Score, Davies-Bouldin Index, Adjusted Rand Index (ARI)

2. **Hierarchical Clustering**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Silhouette Score, Davies-Bouldin Index, Adjusted Rand Index (ARI)

3. **DBSCAN**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Silhouette Score, Davies-Bouldin Index, Adjusted Rand Index (ARI)

4. **Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Silhouette Score, Davies-Bouldin Index, Adjusted Rand Index (ARI), Bayesian Information Criterion (BIC), Akaike Information Criterion (AIC)

### Dimensionality Reduction Models

1. **Principal Component Analysis (PCA)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Explained Variance Ratio, Reconstruction Error

2. **Linear Discriminant Analysis (LDA)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Explained Variance Ratio, Classification Accuracy (when used for classification)

3. **t-Distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding (t-SNE)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Visual inspection (plot quality), Perplexity

4. **Autoencoders**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Reconstruction Error

### Anomaly Detection Models

1. **Isolation Forest**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC

2. **One-Class SVM**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC

3. **Elliptic Envelope**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Precision, Recall, F1 Score, ROC-AUC

### Time Series Models

1. **ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)

2. **SARIMA (Seasonal ARIMA)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)

3. **Prophet**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)

4. **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) Networks**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)

### Recommendation Systems

1. **Collaborative Filtering**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), Precision at K, Recall at K

2. **Content-Based Filtering**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), Precision at K, Recall at K

3. **Matrix Factorization (e.g., SVD, ALS)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), Precision at K, Recall at K

### Reinforcement Learning Models

1. **Q-Learning**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Cumulative Reward, Average Reward

2. **Deep Q-Networks (DQN)**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Cumulative Reward, Average Reward

3. **Policy Gradient Methods**
   - **Evaluation Metrics**: Cumulative Reward, Average Reward

Each metric has its own use case and is selected based on the specific problem, dataset characteristics, and the goals of the analysis. It is often beneficial to evaluate a model using multiple metrics to get a comprehensive understanding of its performance.
